What is Lattice Protocol?

Lattice protocol is building the world's first decentralised architecture for business to collaborate in a seamless and trustless manner, while offering Synergies and creating a strong value network.

Features

Globally Accessible

Lattice is a global network, and though its Distributed architecture will break the geographic siloes of business opportunities. Lattice’s SDK suite will help Businesses and Customers migrate to a decentralized network at a click and access the Global liquidity pool of Business opportunities.

First Decentralized Collaborative Network

Lattice Protocol will be the first of its kind decentralized collaborative model seamlessly facilitating B2B, B2C and C2C collaboration. Built on a 3 tier architecture, the protocol aims to be the future of a smart connected value economy.

Drive Synergies

Lattice Protocol is designed bottom up to help smart Businesses and Customers realize the true essence of synergy and thus assist vertical, horizontal or cross integrations between the network participants, driving value creation.

Curated Positioning

Small businesses in centralized networks often compromises on brand equity to attain customer liquidity. Lattice Protocol aims to offer tailor made audiences to small enterprises, without compromising on Brand equity.

Data Ownership

Lattice respects ones Data privacy and unlike large concentrated network, gives its users an ownership of their data. Lattice aims to break the jinx of data concentration and wealth by a few global giants and return the data possession rights back to its users.

Dynamic Reputation Engine

The protocol will attribute dynamic Reputation scores to its participants, driving Business value through a smart Reputation engine, tied to social constructs and network behavior.

Inbuilt Governance

Lattice Protocol through its inbuilt Governance model will promote information credibility and sanctity of the network. At all levels a dynamic validation engine driven by network participants would keep a watch on the network and its activities and AI/ML models would be leveraged for fraud prevention.

Scalable

Lattice Protocol realizes the scaling opportunity of this model and thus has built a 3 tier architecture running on a combination of On-Chain, Side-Chain and Off-chain logic optimizing cost, speed, and efficiency. Lattice protocol will build its own chain post the first launch, to accommodate bigger Business models.

Trustless

Lattice aims to create a trustless engine, allowing participants to synergize without the regular bottlenecks. Lattice believes the potential of a Business scale is best achieved through coo petition (cooperate to Compete) and thus through its indigenous trust engines, help build a collaborative network.
